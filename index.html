~ About us ~

Welcome to Black Velvet, We love Copenhagen and the city's many possibilities, therefore we strive for our guide website to be able to inspire you who are new to Copenhagen and would like to get to know the city's many gems.
Or for the local Copenhageners who want to be surprised and discover new, unconventional places. Black Velvet is originally a drink that contains the best of both worlds - wine and beer. And that is exactly what our website is all about.
In addition, the food scene here in Copenhagen is something very special. The city is small, but the possibilities are endless. So if you're hungry and your stomach is empty, you've come to the right place!
Enjoy.
