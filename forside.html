About us

We are four multimediadesign students from Copenhagen School of Design and Technology who have been given the task of creating a website with content for our shared passion.
We have created this website, that is a guide, for those of you who love to go out in Copenhagen.
Everything from pubs too restaurants. The name Black Velvet comes from a cocktail, that is mixed with wine and beer, and that’s what this guide is all about.

We hope you like it!
